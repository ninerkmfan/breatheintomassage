A Few Places to Eat/Drink

  • The Post- Our go-to pizza place in town. Delicious NY-style pizzas with great local beers on tap.
  • Bar Vetti- If you're looking for a nice dinner out, make some reservations here. Also convenient if you're staying in the AC Hotel since it's on the ground floor.
  • Guacamole- With a new location in downtown Louisville, come here for guaranteed delicious Mexican food.
  • Jack Fry's- Treat yourself to a classic upscale dinner in this relic that's been opened since the 30's. If you do go, make reservations well in advance and see if you can grab a seat in the bar area.
  • The Eagle- Casual place with fried chicken and other southern fare with outdoor seating in the heart of the Highlands neighborhood.
  • Feast BBQ- Delicious BBQ with counter service with large communal tables.
  • Against the Grain Brewery- Located at Louisville Slugger Field, a ballpark brewery with delicious food.
  • TEN20 Brewery- Delicious beer with food offerings on site! Plenty of indoor and outdoor space for large groups.
  • West 6th NULU- Awesome place to grab some drinks and hang out in the heart of the trendy NULU neighborhood.
